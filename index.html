About Us

Welcome to SkateShop, where passion meets pavement in the heart of Riverside, California!
As a family-owned skate shop, we take pride in being more than just a store
We're a tight-knit community that shares a love for skateboarding and the vibrant culture that surrounds it.

What's The Mission?

Established in 2024, our journey began with a simple mission: to provide the best skate gear and create a hub for skateboard enthusiasts in Riverside.
We've grown into a go-to destination for skaters of all levels, offering a curated selection of top-quality boards, gear, and apparel from the most respected brands in the industry.

The Team

At SkateShop, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported us throughout the years. That's why we proudly sponsor a small but mighty local skate team. These talented individuals embody the spirit of Riverside skate culture, and we are honored to support their passion and dedication to the sport.

Join Our Community

Join us at SkateShop
Your home for all things skateboarding in Riverside. Whether you're looking for the latest gear, seeking advice, or simply want to connect with like-minded individuals, we invite you to be a part of our skate family. Skate on!
